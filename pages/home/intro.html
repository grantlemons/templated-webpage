I’m Grant, a passionate software engineer working at Credera. I'm a huge proponent of free software, and the copyleft licensing that often accompanies it.

Improvement is extremely important to me, so I am constantly challenging myself by using tools with steep learning curves that emphasize power over accessibility or ease of use.

Don't confuse this for the whole productivity culture thing that prioritizes efficiency over all else. The most important thing for me is the process. While I like accomplishing things, what I enjoy most is spending a whole day in a flow state working on software.
